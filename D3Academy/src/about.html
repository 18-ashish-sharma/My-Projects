About Dance

All type of Dance

I have done senior diploma in Kathak and have been teaching dance for the past 12 years. I run my own dance academy and work as a choreographer. I have worked with the Salsa choreographer Sandeep Soparkar and have been a jury to many fashion and dance shows. I have won awards for social works and in training children, and now I am taking online classes since one year. I am looking forward to meeting some really amazing and keen to learn students/people with whom I can share my experiences and knowledge.

Yoga
